# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 18, 2026**

## Overview

Stockholm T-Minus is a transit departure app for Stockholm's public transportation system. This privacy policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect:** Your device's location coordinates (latitude and longitude)
- **How we use it:** To find nearby transit stops when you use the "find stops near me" feature
- **Storage:** Location data is used only in the moment and is NOT stored, transmitted to our servers, or shared with third parties

### Transit Preferences
- **What we collect:** Your selected transit stops and line filters
- **Storage:** This data is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted anywhere

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
- Account information (we don't require accounts)
- Usage analytics or tracking
- Advertising identifiers

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party service:

1. **SL (Storstockholms Lokaltrafik) API** - To fetch transit departure times

## Data Security

All network communications use HTTPS encryption. Local data is stored in Android's secure app storage.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Deny location permissions (manual stop search will still work)
- Uninstall the app to delete all local data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
